    About me...



    General Information:


    Hello and thank you for visiting my website.

    My name is David, but most people know me as Purple or Purple Foundation. I am an eighteen-year-old video game live streamer and video game enthusiast who lives in Wales.

    I live stream five times a week for around five hours at a time and you can find me at twitch.tv

    My live streams are focused around speedrunning.
    Speedrunning is trying to beat a game as fast as possible and there are thousands of games and types of speedruns available.

    My current game that I play most frequently while live streaming is The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie, which I also currently hold the record for in the Any% category.



    Frequently Asked Questions:


    Q: Why don't you stream practice?

    A: Practice is boring and I'd rather focus as much as possible while practicing

    Q: Do you speedrun Battle For Bikini Bottom?

    A: I used to but I'm pretty disinterested in the game now that it's completely different from when I started playing in 2016

    Q: How many hours do you have in The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie?

    A: ~3000

    Q: Do you only stream The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie?

    A: Yes, almost exclusively any% but I'll be more willing to play other categories once I achieve 1:07

    Q: How did you fix your hand pain?

    A: A very informative video helped me.

    Q: Is The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie difficult to learn?

    A: Not at all. There are only a couple tricks that beginners need to do that could cause some issues. Thanks to Mindy skip, the game has never been easier to learn

    Q: What are "the big three"?

    A: Floating block skip, GESK jumps and Fast corkscrew are the hardest tricks in this game and save a maximum of 35s, 19s and 10s respectively (ordered from easiest to hardest)
